Hello!! I’m Carolyn AKA Tinky. My husband and I own Pixie Dusted Memories Travel. I’ve been selling travel for about 10 years now. I love all things Disney and have been to Disneyland at least a thousand times, WDW several times, and have done 10 Disney Cruises! Back in 2005 I planned a trip to WDW and a Cruise on the Disney Magic for my family of 15. We had multiple people with various disabilities from mobility, diabetes, ADHD, Sleep Apnea, Dementia, Food Allergies, along with Vision and Hearing loss. The AAA agent we were working with was not helpful with figuring out what we needed for those with special needs! Her response was to tell me to, “Call Disney or search the website.” I learned a lot about traveling with disabilities and from then on often reach out to help others trying to travel with disabilities. I told myself if I ever became a Travel Agent, I would pay special attention to those with disabilities. Over the years I’ve helped those with all kinds of disabilities travel and I’m here to help many more.
